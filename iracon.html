Examples from popular culture

E(7,12) = [x . x x . x . x x . x .] is a common West African bell pattern. For example, it is used in the Mpr e rhythm of the Ashanti people of Ghana.

If you rotate it by 3 beats, you get:

[x . x . x x . x . x . x]

which I was sure was in a Dawn of Midi tune, but is not.
